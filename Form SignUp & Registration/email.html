About the Speaker

Dr. Carlos Lopez-Mariscal is the Chief Scientific Officer at Underwater Photonics Inc. and Research Professor of Physics at the Department of Physics and Astronomy at the Appalachian State University. He obtained BSc, MSc, and PhD degrees from Tecnologico de Monterrey in Monterrey, Mexico and worked as a postdoctoral researcher at the Center for Nanoscale Science and Technology of the U.S. National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST). He developed proprietary automation technology for optically tracking and sorting large samples for biological specimens, including airborne biological warfare agents and human bloodborne pathogens at the Bio-Analytical Chemistry Division of the U.S. Naval Research Laboratory (NRL).

Dr. Lopez-Mariscal has served as a member of the Board of Directors of The Optical Society of America (OSA). He is an OSA Senior Member as well as a member of the inaugural class of OSA Ambassadors. As a part of his volunteer work, he is an active OSA Travelling Lecturer and he regularly talks about professional development at research and educational institutions worldwide.
